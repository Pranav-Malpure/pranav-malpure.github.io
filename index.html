About Me

I am a fourth year undergraduate student in the Aerospace Department at IIT Bombay. My research interests lie in robotics, UAVs and Controls. Currently I am working on project aiming to improve dexterous object gripping by manipulators. I like to solve hard problems and learn something new!

Projects (Incomplete List)

The Humanoid Project

Leading a Humanoid team at college which can help sort books in the library and identify books placed in wrong shelves. Responsible for integrating the tasks of various subsystems - AI, Electronics, Controls and Robot Design.

Force based dexterous manipulation

Researching about RL controlled manipulation to be used to identify and grip basic objects like cylinders, based on image as well as force feedback

Autonomous underwater navigation

Programmed RexROV2 to autonomously navigate underwater using curvature velocity method to avoid obstacles. Simulated in Gazebo

Visibility Graphs(Flytbase Labs)

Devised a novel technique to optimize the addition of new polygons in existing visibility graphs for drones. Reduced the recalculation time by around 92%.
