About Me

I am an undergraduate student in the Aerospace Department at IIT Bombay. My research interests lie in robotics, UAVs and Controls. I want to make a positive impact in the world and working towards it.

Projects

The Humanoid Project

Developing a Humanoid which can help sort books in the library. Working in the controls and designing subsystem.

Quadruped

Building a quadruped which is able to walk in certain direction based on camera input. Work includes finding equations of motion, designing controller, trajectory planning, and manufacturing it.

Autonomous landing of UAVs on Moving surface

Designing a controller to achieve autonomous landing by quadrotor on a surface using Aruco markers.

Autonomous underwater navigation

Autonomously navigated an underwater vehicle using curvature velocity method to avoid obstacles.

Multi-Robot Graph exploration

Researched a novel method to explore a cyclic graph in the least possible time using multiple robots, through their optimal placement. It involved using the already existing MR-DFS method.

Network graph based reasoning

Developed an algorithm based on network graphs which has ability of reasoning. It can answer queries based on random facts passed to the system.
